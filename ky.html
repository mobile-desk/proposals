About Sweets and Treats by Ky

Founded in 2022, Sweets and Treats by Ky brings a magical, futuristic twist to traditional baking. Our passion is creating desserts that are not only delicious but visually stunning and unique.

Every creation is handcrafted with love and attention to detail, using only the finest ingredients. We specialize in custom designs that bring your sweetest dreams to life, whether for birthdays, weddings, or any special occasion.

Our signature holographic designs and pastel color palette have become our trademark, making each dessert not just a treat for the taste buds but a feast for the eyes.
